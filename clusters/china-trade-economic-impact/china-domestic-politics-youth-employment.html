Description

This subcluster examines China's internal political dynamics and structural economic challenges, particularly youth unemployment and domestic governance issues. Articles analyze CCP leadership changes, military appointments, political purges, and censorship campaigns alongside critical employment data showing youth joblessness reaching nearly 19%. Coverage includes government statistics, policy announcements, and analysis of social control measures. Unlike the trade-focused sibling subcluster examining China's external economic relationships, this grouping concentrates on domestic stability concerns, including TikTok regulatory battles that reflect broader US-China tensions over technology and influence.
