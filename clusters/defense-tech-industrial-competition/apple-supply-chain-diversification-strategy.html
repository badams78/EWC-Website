Description

This subcluster examines Apple's strategic shift away from China-centric manufacturing toward geographic diversification, particularly emphasizing India as an alternative production hub. Articles track quantitative metrics like production percentages, investment figures, and year-over-year growth rates, drawing from industry analyses and corporate disclosures. Coverage focuses on operational logistics, supplier relocations, and automation strategies rather than military applications. Unlike sibling subclusters addressing semiconductor competition or defense spending, this group concentrates on commercial supply chain resilience and market access considerations, positioning Apple's manufacturing decisions within broader US-China economic decoupling trends.
