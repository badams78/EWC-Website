Description

This subcluster examines broad patterns in income distribution and earnings inequality across different demographic groups and time periods. Articles analyze long-term wage trends, particularly comparing outcomes between college-educated and non-college workers, generational wealth shifts, and earnings trajectories across income quintiles. Common data sources include Federal Reserve analysis, Bureau of Labor Statistics longitudinal data, and academic research spanning 1960-2022. The focus emphasizes macroeconomic inequality trends rather than specific educational interventions or targeted demographic disparities, distinguishing it from subclusters examining particular populations or policy effectiveness.
