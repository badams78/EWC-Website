Description

This subcluster examines the rapid expansion of global electricity demand driven by datacenter growth, renewable energy transition, and industrial electrification. Articles feature quantitative forecasts from organizations like IEA and Goldman Sachs, tracking metrics such as generation capacity additions, investment flows, and demand growth rates across major economies. The content emphasizes China's massive infrastructure buildout versus slower US transmission development, while analyzing how carbon reduction policies influence industrial competitiveness. Unlike the sibling subcluster focusing on US energy production and trade flows, this cluster takes a broader international perspective on power sector investment trends and infrastructure capacity planning.
