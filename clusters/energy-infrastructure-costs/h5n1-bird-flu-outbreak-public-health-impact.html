Description

This subcluster focuses on the H5N1 avian influenza outbreak's escalation and public health implications, tracking viral transmission patterns across animal populations and human cases. Articles predominantly feature epidemiological data from CDC analyses, WHO assessments, and surveillance reports documenting viral spread through dairy cattle, poultry, and emerging human infections. Content emphasizes genomic sequencing results, serological surveys, and variant identification like the D1.1 strain. Unlike sibling subclusters examining energy production dynamics or infrastructure investments, this collection prioritizes infectious disease monitoring, zoonotic transmission pathways, and pandemic preparedness concerns within agricultural sectors.
