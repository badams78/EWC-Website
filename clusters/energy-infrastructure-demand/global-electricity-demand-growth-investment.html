Description

This subcluster examines the rapidly accelerating global electricity demand driven by digitalization, industrial growth, and energy transition. Articles focus on quantitative projections showing 40-50% demand growth by 2035, with particular emphasis on data center power consumption and renewable energy deployment. Content draws from industry forecasts, government statistics, and investment analyses, comparing regional approaches especially between China's massive capacity additions and US infrastructure investment patterns. Unlike sibling subclusters focused on US energy production leadership, this collection emphasizes demand-side pressures and the infrastructure investment response across global markets, highlighting the gap between projected electricity needs and current grid capacity expansion rates.
