Description

This subcluster examines the resurgence of nuclear power and breakthrough developments in fusion technology as critical components of future energy infrastructure. Articles analyze cost comparisons between nuclear, gas, and renewable technologies, highlighting nuclear's current $15/watt disadvantage versus $1/watt alternatives. Coverage includes private fusion investment trends, with companies like Helion and Commonwealth Fusion Systems securing billions in funding, alongside geothermal innovations from firms like Fervo. Content draws from industry reports, government policy announcements, and startup funding data. Unlike sibling clusters focused on conventional energy exports or broad electricity demand, this subcluster specifically tracks emerging nuclear technologies and their commercial viability timelines.
