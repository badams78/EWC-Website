Description

This subcluster examines the relationship between government fiscal imbalances and sovereign debt sustainability, focusing on how mounting deficits affect long-term borrowing costs. Articles analyze deficit projections, debt-to-GDP ratios, and policy recommendations from institutions like the CBO, IMF, BIS, and Federal Reserve banks. Coverage includes cross-country comparisons of fiscal positions, off-balance-sheet liabilities, and debt maturity strategies. Unlike the sibling subcluster on Treasury Market Demand which focuses on investor behavior and auction mechanics, this cluster emphasizes the fundamental fiscal drivers behind yield movements and sustainability thresholds that influence bond market dynamics.
