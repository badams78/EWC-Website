Description

This subcluster examines Treasury market dynamics through the lens of foreign investment flows and international yield relationships. Articles analyze forward yield curves (10y10y, 10y20y), cross-border capital movements, and comparative sovereign debt markets, particularly Japan-US yield interactions and Bank of Japan intervention effects. Content draws heavily from market data, Federal Reserve bank holdings, and international debt-to-GDP ratios. The focus spans from technical yield curve analysis to broader questions of foreign demand for US Treasuries and global bond market interconnectedness. Unlike domestic-focused Treasury analysis, this subcluster emphasizes international perspectives and cross-market spillover effects on long-term US rates.
