Description

This subcluster examines the intersection of market concentration trends and technology-driven investment returns in modern economies. Articles analyze corporate markup increases, equity valuations using metrics like CAPE ratios, and AI's impact on firm performance and productivity. Common data sources include academic studies on market power, financial analytics from major institutions like Goldman Sachs, and corporate performance metrics spanning decades. The focus integrates empirical research on industrial concentration with investment analysis, particularly examining how technological transformation affects market structure and returns. Coverage spans venture capital performance, corporate financing patterns, and valuation adjustments for intangible assets in tech-heavy markets.
